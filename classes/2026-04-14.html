Class 05: How Question Word & Intro to Past Simple (Daily Routine)

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-04-14
  • Topic: How Question Word & Intro to Past Simple (Daily Routine)

Objetivo

Consolidar o uso da Question Word How e introduzir as bases do Past Simple (Affirmative, Negative, Interrogative) utilizando verbos regulares e irregulares aplicados à rotina diária.

1. Warm-up & Review (How)

Usamos How para perguntar o “como” (modo) ou quantidades.

  • How are you? (Como você está?)

  • How many employees do you have? (Quantos funcionários você tem?)

Term (EN) Translation (PT)
Employer Empregador / Patrão
Employee Funcionário / Colaborador
Employed Empregado (Verbo/Estado)

2. Past Simple: The Basics

Affirmative (+)

Para verbos regulares, adicionamos -ED:

  • I worked for her.

  • She worked for that company.

Common Irregular Verbs

Estes mudam a forma e exigem prática constante:

  • Go → Went

  • Have → Had

  • Do → Did

  • Make → Made

  • Run → Ran

  • Read → Read (Som de “Red”)

  • Is/Am → Was / Are → Were

Negative (-)

Usamos didn’t + verbo na forma base (sem o “ED”):

  • I didn’t do the homework.

  • She didn’t work yesterday.

Interrogative (?)

Usamos Did no início da frase:

  • Did you go to the grocery store?

  • Yes, I went yesterday!

3. Practice: Monica’s Routine (Yesterday)

Relatando as ações de ontem em ordem cronológica:

  1. I went to the gym at 5am.

  2. At 6am I made my breakfast.

  3. 7am I studied English with teacher Jeronimo.

  4. Right after, I went to work and stayed there until 5pm.

  5. At night I made dinner and watched TV.

  6. At 10pm I went to bed.

4. Vocabulary & Pronunciation

  • Right after / Shortly after: Logo em seguida / Logo após.

  • Bedroom: O quarto (cômodo).

  • Bed vs. Bad: Cuidado com o som do “E” (curto) e do “A” (aberto).

  • Stayed: Usado para “permanecer” em um lugar (ex: no trabalho).
