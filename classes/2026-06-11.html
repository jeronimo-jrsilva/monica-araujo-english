🔬 1. Phrasal Verbs for PMs

Mastering the verbs of action and investigation.

The “Audit” List: 1. Carry out: Executar / Cumprir. - “The officer carried out an order from the judge.” - Pronúncia: Carry (/ˈkæri/ - carregar) vs Care (/kɛər/ - cuidado). 2. Look into: Investigar / Analisar. - “She looked into his phone.” 3. Call off: Cancelar. - “The meeting was called off.” - “The board called off the meeting.” 4. Hold up: Atrasar / Reter. - “The meeting had to be held up.” - Diferença: Hold up (atrasar) vs Hold on (persistir/aguardar). 5. Iron out: Resolver pequenos detalhes / “Passar a limpo”. - “We need to iron out the hospital project’s details.” - Pronúncia: Iron (/ˈaɪərn/ - como ‘aiarn’).

🔑 2. Possessive Adjectives & ’S

Reviewing the ownership structure: - My / Your / His / Her / Its / Our / Your / Their - The ’S Structure (Subject’s Object): - “This is Jane’s house.” - “The company’s budget.” - “My uncle’s boat.”

⚡ 3. Advanced Causative Scenarios (Delegation)

Structure: HAVE + SOMETHING + DONE (V3)

Cenário Ação (Delegada) Vocabulário Chave
Biohazard Leak “I’ll have the area isolated.” Leak (vazamento), Isolate (isolar).
X-Ray Lead “I’ll have the shielding replaced.” Shield (escudo), Shielding (isolamento/blindagem).
ICU Air Filtration “I’ll have the filters checked.” High (alto) vs Low (baixo).
Compliance Audit “I’ll have the signs reprinted.” Wrong (errado), Language (idioma).

Note on Loss vs Lost: - Loss (perda / prejuízo). - Lost (perdido).

🏗️ 4. Vocabulary & Technical Terms

  • Subcontractor: Empreiteiro / Terceirizado.
  • Issue: Problema / Contratempo.
  • Repair / Fix / Patch: Consertar / Remendar.
    • Contexto: Patch panel (TI), Patchwork (trabalho de retalhos).
