[!abstract] Transbordo da Aula 36 Phrasal Verbs de gestão e auditoria que não foram vistos na aula de sábado.

🎯 1. Warm-up: The Delegation Audit

Prompt: "Yesterday you decided to delegate 3 tasks. Tell me what you had done."
Example: "I had the HVAC filters replaced."

🔬 2. Phrasal Verbs for Project Managers

Objective: Mastering the verbs of action and investigation.

The "Audit" List:

  1. Carry out: Executar.
    • "We need to carry out a site inspection before the audit."
  2. Look into: Investigar / Analisar.
    • "I'll look into the budget discrepancy immediately."
  3. Call off: Cancelar.
    • "The site visit was called off due to the heavy rain."
  4. Hold up: Atrasar.
    • "The permit is holding up the entire plumbing installation."
  5. To iron out: Resolver pequenos detalhes.
    • "We need to iron out the last few issues in the floor plan."

Drill Matrix:

  • "Execute the inspection" -> "Carry out the inspection."
  • "Investigate the leak" -> "Look into the leak."
  • "Delay the project" -> "Hold up the project."

⚡ 3. Advanced Causative Scenarios: The Hospital Crisis

Objective: Rapid delegation under pressure.

Scenario 1: Infection Control

  • "You found a biohazard leak in the hallway."
  • Action: "I will have the area isolated and have the biohazard bins emptied."

Scenario 2: Technical Failure

  • "The lead shielding in the X-ray room is compromised."
  • Action: "I must have the shielding replaced before the government audition."

🏗️ 4. Precision Review: Relative Clauses

Prompt: "Define these items using WHO/WHICH/THAT."

  • A Subcontractor: "A person who..."
  • A Blueprint: "A document which..."
  • A Bottleneck: "A situation that..."
  • The Board: "The group who..."

🏆 5. Final Challenge: The Pre-Audit Briefing

Goal: Prepare the team for the "Government Audition".
Requirements:

  • Use 2 Phrasal Verbs (Carry out / Look into).
  • Use 2 Causative Forms (Have something done).
  • Use 1 Contrast connector (Despite).
