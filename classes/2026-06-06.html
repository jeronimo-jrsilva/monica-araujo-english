Topic: Precision & Delegation: Relative Clauses & Causative Form

Mastering Relative Clauses for precision and Causative Form for delegation in healthcare design.

1. Precision: Relative Clauses

  • Who: For people (The manager who...).
  • Which/That: For things/documents (The blueprint which...).

2. Delegation: Causative Form

Structure: HAVE + SOMETHING + DONE

  • "I'll have the area isolated."
  • "I'll have the plans updated."
