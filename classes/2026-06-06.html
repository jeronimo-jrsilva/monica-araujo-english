Class 36: Precision & Delegation: Relative Clauses, Causative Form & Contrast Connectors

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-06-06
  • Topic: Precision & Delegation: Relative Clauses, Causative Form & Contrast Connectors

Foco em precisão absoluta para relatórios e comando executivo. Injeção de vocabulário de infraestrutura e normas sanitárias.

1. Warm-up: The Regulatory Obstacle (Contrast Mastery)

Drill Matrix (Contrast + Infrastructure):

  1. Although the fire regulations were strict… -> Despite the strict fire regulations…

  2. Although the ceiling height was insufficient… -> Despite the insufficient ceiling height.

  3. Although the material was expensive… -> Despite the expensive material, we could finish the construction within budget.

  4. Although the deadline is tight… -> Despite the tight deadline, compliance is mandatory.

Vocabulary Boost:

  • Can / Could: Poder, conseguir.

  • Be able to: Conseguir (requer conjugação do to be).

  • Height /haɪt/: Altura.

  • High /haɪ/: Alto.

  • Low /loʊ/: Baixo.

  • Ceiling /ˈsiːlɪŋ/: Teto / Pé direito.

  • Compliance: Conformidade; obediência a normas/leis.

  • Backlog: Tarefas acumuladas/pendentes.

  • Bottleneck: Gargalo.

  • Blueprints: Plantas técnicas.

  • Government audition: Auditoria governamental.

2. Technical Injection: Relative Clauses (Precision)

Objective: Eliminate ambiguity in technical descriptions.

Advanced Drills (Architecture & Health):

  1. “He is the subcontractor. He is responsible for the HVAC system.” -> “He is the subcontractor WHO is responsible for the HVAC system.”

  2. “That is the antibacterial coating. It was applied to the operating room.” -> “That is the antibacterial coating WHICH/THAT was applied to the operating room.”

  3. “Those are the biohazard bins. They must be emptied every 4 hours.” -> “Those are the biohazard bins WHICH/THAT must be emptied every 4 hours.”

  4. “The workflow is efficient. It was designed by the board.” -> “This is the efficient workflow THAT was designed by the board.” / “That is the board WHO designed the efficient workflow.”

Spatial Vocabulary:

  • this: perto, singular.

  • that: longe, singular.

  • these: perto, plural.

  • those: longe, plural.

  • Biohazard: Risco biológico.

  • Empty / Emptied: Vazio / Esvaziadas.

  • Workflow: Fluxo de trabalho.

  • Board: Mesa diretora / Conselho.

3. Executive Layer: The Causative Form (Delegation)

Scenario: Managing vs Doing. Focus on accountability.

Structure: SUB + HAVE/GET (flexionado) + OBJECT + PART (3rd col)

Transformation (From Doing to Delegating):

  1. “I’ll check the plumbing.” -> “I’ll have the plumbing checked.”

  2. “I’m painting the hallway.” -> “I’m having the hallway painted.”

  3. “I signed the permit.” -> “I had the permit signed.”

  4. “I’ll install the lead shielding for the X-ray room.” -> “I’ll have the lead shielding for the X-ray room installed.”

Vocabulary:

  • Lead /led/: Chumbo.

  • Shield / Shielding: Escudo / Proteção.

  • Signed: Sinalizado / Assinado.
