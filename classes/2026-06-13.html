Class 38: The Drill Edition - Precision & Rapid Delegation

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-06-13
  • Topic: The Drill Edition: Precision & Rapid Delegation

Mecanização do Causative Form e expansão de Relative Clauses através de drillings intensivos em múltiplos cenários técnicos. A aluna deve construir 100% das frases baseada em gatilhos.

1. The Causative Machine (Rapid-Fire Drill)

Objective: Instant delegation using HAVE + SOMETHING + DONE.

Trigger (Problem) Action (Delegated) Vocabulary
"The hospital elevator is stuck." "I'll have it fixed." Stuck
"The floor tiles are cracked." "I'll have them replaced." Cracked
"The budget was rejected." "I'll have it revised." Revised
"The biohazard signs are missing." "I'll have them reprinted." Reprinted
"The HVAC filters are dirty." "I'll have them cleaned." Filters

2. The Precision Builder (Relative Clauses)

Objective: Merging simple sentences into high-density executive definitions using WHO / WHICH / THAT.

  • Scenario 1: "He is the subcontractor who repairs the HVAC in the ICU."
  • Scenario 2: "This is the blueprint which the board approved."
  • Scenario 3: "It is a bottleneck that delays the project progress."
  • Scenario 4: "This is the punch list which we need to finish before delivery."

3. Scenario React: The Crisis Briefing

Objective: Managing mixed scenarios using Phrasal Verbs and Connectors.

Context: Government Audit vs. New Office

Practice reacting to these triggers using LOOK INTO, CARRY OUT, ALTHOUGH and DESPITE.

4. Vocabulary Mastery

  • Punch List: Lista de pendências finais.
  • To Brief: Dar instruções essenciais.
  • Lead Time: Prazo de entrega / Tempo total de espera.
  • Stuck / Cracked / Revised: Preso / Rachado / Revisado.
