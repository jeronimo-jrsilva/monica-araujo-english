Class 38: The Rapid-Fire Special - Automatic Mastery

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-06-13
  • Topic: The Rapid-Fire Special: Automatic Mastery

O objetivo hoje não é "aprender" e sim "automatizar". A aluna não deve ter tempo de pensar na regra, apenas em reagir ao cenário técnico através de máquinas de drilling.

1. The Causative Machine (Infrastructure)

Trigger (Problem) Expected Action (Delegated)
"The security cameras are blurry." "I'll have them cleaned."
"The blueprints are outdated." "I'll have them updated."
"The fire alarm is beeping." "I'll have it checked."
"The paint is peeling in the lobby." "I'll have it repainted."

2. The Time Machine (Past vs. Present Perfect)

Check for time markers: Specific Time (Past) vs. Relevance/Bridge (Present Perfect).

  • "Did you finish the report yesterday?" (Specific Time)
  • "Have you visited the site recently?" (Unspecified/Bridge)
  • "We fixed the leak two hours ago." (Specific Time)
  • "I have never seen this type of HVAC." (Life Experience)

3. The Compliance Machine (Modals)

  • "A worker is without a hard hat." → "He MUST wear it."
  • "The emergency exit is blocked." → "We SHOULD clear it."
  • "A visitor wants to enter the ICU." → "He CAN'T enter."

4. The Logical Machine (Conditionals)

  • "If the audit starts now, we will be in trouble." (Real Possibility)
  • "If we had more budget, we would hire more people." (Hypothetical)
  • "If the elevator fails tomorrow, I will call the subcontractor." (Prediction)

5. Final Challenge: The Multi-Task CEO

Manage 3 simultaneous crises using the grammar from the machines above.
