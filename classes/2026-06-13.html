Topic: Precision in Reporting & The Pre-Audit Challenge

Refining descriptive precision using Relative Clauses and consolidating executive delegation.

1. Relative Clauses Precision

Defining technical roles and documents with WHO / WHICH / THAT.

  • A Subcontractor is a person who repairs the HVAC.
  • A Blueprint is a document which shows the design.
  • A Bottleneck is a situation that delays progress.
  • A Punch List is a list which contains remaining tasks.

2. The Final Challenge

The Pre-Audit Briefing: Combine Phrasal Verbs, Causative Form, and Contrast Connectors (Despite/Although) to brief your team.
