🏗️ Foundations: The Architecture of Comparison

Usamos comparativos para estabelecer relação entre dois elementos e superlativos para destacar o topo de uma categoria.

⚖️ A. Comparatives (A vs B)

Structure: Noun 1 + (to be) + [Adjective + er / More + Adjective] + THAN + Noun 2

  • "The left side building is bigger than the right side building."
  • "I'm taller than the average woman."
  • "I'm smaller than the reference for women."
  • "Argentinian wine is worse than Chilean wine." (Irregular: Bad → Worse).
  • "Steel is more resistant than plastic."

🏔️ Superlatives: The Best in Class

Structure: The + [Adjective + est / Most + Adjective]

  • "The tallest mountain is Everest."
  • "What is the shortest way to Carrefour?" (Shortest path/route).
  • "Glass is better than wood, but steel is the best."
  • "Turin is better than Napoli, but Rome is the best."

🗣️ Technical Drills & Phonetics

Monica, foco nestas variações de adjetivos curtos:

  • Big → Bigger → The Biggest
  • Small → Smaller → The Smallest
  • Tall → Taller → The Tallest
  • Cheap → Cheaper → The Cheapest
  • Pretty → Prettier → The Prettiest
