2. Tense Consolidation: The Architect’s Timeline

Tense Use Case Example (Technical & Personal)
Past Simple Completed tasks “We designed the floor plan last week.”
Present Simple Facts / Regulations “The hospital requires accessibility ramps.”
Present Continuous Current actions “The team is updating the CAD files now.”
Future (Going to) Planned steps “We are going to present the budget on Monday.”
Future (Will) Decisions/Predictions “I will call the contractor to fix this.”

3. Grammar Focus: Future Structures

A. Future Simple (Will)

  • Affirmative: Subject + WILL + Verb

  • Negative: Subject + WON'T + Verb

  • Interrogative: WILL + Subject + Verb?

B. Future (Going to)

  • Affirmative: Subject + TO BE + GOING TO + Verb

  • Negative: Subject + NOT + TO BE + GOING TO + Verb

  • Interrogative: TO BE + Subject + GOING TO + Verb?
