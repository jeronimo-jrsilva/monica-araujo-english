🎯 Executive Summary

Nesta aula, consolidamos os conceitos de Vastu Shastra através de exercícios intensivos de Drilling. Focamos na integração de Passive Voice (Voz Passiva) com verbos modais e no uso preciso de Relative Clauses para descrições técnicas. Também exploramos o vocabulário de áreas de serviço (Laundry/Toilet).

🧠 1. Grammar Drilling: Technical Precision

Monica, focamos na fluidez ao descrever processos e normas:

🔵 Passive Voice + Modals (Directives)

  • "The main entrance should be placed in the North or East quadrant."
  • "Negative energy can be blocked by using thick walls and stone cladding in the South."
  • "The plinth needs to be designed carefully to ensure stability."

🟢 Relative Clauses & Identifying (One/Ones)

  • "The Brahmsthan is the area that/which is located at the center of the house."
  • "Of the two façades, the North one is the one which provides more light."
  • "The poison arrows are the ones that we must avoid."

🏗️ 2. Real-World Application

Convertendo frases do seu projeto para a voz passiva:

  • Active: "We located the toilet at the entrance of the house."
  • Passive: "The toilet was located at the entrance of the house."
  • Present Perfect: "The toilet has been located at the entrance..."
