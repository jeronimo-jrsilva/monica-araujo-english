🧠 Warm-up & Vocabulary Enrichment

  • Migraine: Enxaqueca.
  • Tough: Pesado, duro, complicado.
  • Leak / Leaking: Vazar / Vazamento (Ex: "There were leakings at some fixtures.").
  • Sink: Pia (também o verbo "afundar").
  • Corner: Canto (Cuidado com a pronúncia para não parecer Corn - milho).

🛠️ Technical Grammar: The Preposition Pyramid

AT (Specific Points & Intersections)

  • At the entrance: Na entrada.
  • At the construction site: No canteiro de obras.
  • At the corner of the room: No canto da sala.
  • At the junction: No cruzamento/junção.

ON (Surfaces & Floors)

  • On the floor / On the roof: No chão / No telhado.
  • On the second floor: No segundo andar.
  • On the wall: Na parede.
  • On the ceiling: No teto.
  • On the table: Na mesa (clássico).

IN (Enclosed Spaces & Areas)

  • In the operating room: Na sala de cirurgia.
  • In the storage area: Na área de estoque.
  • In the blueprint: No desenho técnico / Planta.
  • Made in Brazil: Fabricado no Brasil.

🏗️ Spatial Relationships (Contextual)

  • Beside / Next to: Ao lado de.
  • Between: Entre (2 itens) / Among: Entre (muitos).
  • Opposite / Across from: Do outro lado / Oposto a.
  • Under / Below / Beneath: Abaixo / Sob.
  • Above / Over: Acima / Sobre.
  • Behind / In the back of: Atrás.
  • In front of: Em frente a.

🗣️ Real World Examples (From Class)

  • "Where is the emergency exit in the blueprint?"
  • "The furniture specification must be in the layout."
  • "There were leakings at some fixtures."
