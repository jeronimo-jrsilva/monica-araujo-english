Class 13: Maintenance & Hypotheses

Date: 2026-04-24

Topics: Hypothetical Scenarios (Second Conditional) & Professional Experience (Present Perfect Deep Dive)

1. Second Conditional (Hypotheses)

Used for imaginary or unlikely situations. If + Past Simple, would + Verb.

  • "If I were the manager, I would change the HVAC provider."
  • "If we had more budget, we would install HEPA filters."

2. Present Perfect (Professional Authority)

Connects past actions to the present impact. Focus on Experience and Status.

  • Since/For: "I have worked as an architect for 15 years."
  • Already/Yet: "I have already finished the draft."

3. Technical Vocabulary

  • HVAC: Climatização.
  • Compliance: Conformidade.
  • Redundancy: Redundância de sistemas.
