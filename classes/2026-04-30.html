1. Practice & Review (Conditionals & Present Perfect)

  1. If the project followed the standards, it would be much safer.

    • follow - seguir
    • safe - seguro / safer - mais seguro
    • laws - leis / rules - regras

  2. I have never dealt with such a tight deadline before.

    • deal / dealt - lidar / lidei
    • such - tal / tanto
    • tight - apertado / estreito

  3. Has she implemented the LEED standards in the maternity ward?

    • LEED - Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design
    • ward - setor / ala

  4. If we lived in a different city, we would design sustainable schools.

  5. I have just found some errors in the inclusion policy draft.

    • find / found - encontrar
    • draft - rascunho
    • search / look for - procurar

2. Study Text: “The San Lucas Clinic Expansion”

Paragraph 1: The Background

“As an architect, I have worked on many healthcare projects since 2010. Last year, my team designed the new wing of the San Lucas Clinic. We finished the blueprints in June, and the construction started right after.”

  • Grammar Note: Contrast between Present Perfect (career duration since 2010) and Past Simple (specific events in June/Last year).
