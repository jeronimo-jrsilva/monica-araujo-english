🛠️ Technical Grammar: Too vs. Enough

Monica, estas estruturas medem o excesso e a suficiência em projetos:

  • TOO + ADJ: "The glare is too strong."
  • ADJ + ENOUGH: "Is the light bright enough?"
  • ENOUGH + NOUN: "We don't have enough fixtures."
  • TOO MUCH / MANY: "Too much water" vs. "Too many children."
