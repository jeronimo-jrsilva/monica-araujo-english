🚀 Grammar: Too vs. Enough

1. TOO + Adjective (Excesso)

  • "The glare is too strong."
  • "This is too good to be true."

Advanced: Too much (uncountable) vs. Too many (countable).

2. Adjective + ENOUGH (Suficiência)

  • "Is the light bright enough?"
  • "The sensors weren't fast enough."

3. ENOUGH + Noun

  • "We don't have enough fixtures for the whole corridor."

👄 Pronunciation & Homophones

  • Whole (/hoʊl/) - Todo/Inteiro vs. Hole (/hoʊl/) - Buraco.
  • Enough (/ɪˈnʌf/) - Termina com som de 'F'.
  • Trouble (/ˈtrʌbl/) - Encrenca / Problema.
