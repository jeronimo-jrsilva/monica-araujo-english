Class 02: Negative & Interrogative (Daily Life)

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-04-09
  • Topic: Present Simple (Negative & Interrogative)

🏗️ Negative Form (Don't / Doesn't)

Structure: Subject + do/does + NOT + Verb (Base form)

  • "I don't like to wash the dishes."
  • "She doesn't like to wear skirts."
  • "If I didn't like to be an architect, I would be a chef."
  • "My daughter doesn't like vegetables."

❓ Interrogative Form (Do / Does)

Structure: Do/Does + Subject + Verb (Base form)?

  • "Do you like to go to the gym?"
  • "Does your daughter like salad?"
  • "Does she prefer the heat or the cold?"

🗣️ Vocabulary & Expressions

  • Wash the dishes: Lavar a louça
  • Iron the clothes: Passar a roupa
  • Furniture: Móveis
  • Tidy up / Messy: Arrumar / Bagunçado
  • Go to the gym / Work out: Ir à academia / Malhar
  • Take a walk / Go for a walk: Dar um passeio / Caminhar
  • Extreme sports: Skydiving / Parachuting (Paraquedismo) / Zipline (Tirolesa)
  • Climate: Heat / Cold / Chill (Calor / Frio / Frescor)
  • Traits: Características / Traços de personalidade

📝 Common Mistakes & Corrections

  • Chicken vs. Kitchen: Cuidado com a pronúncia (Frango vs. Cozinha)
  • Cough vs. Coffee: Diferenciação de sons (Tosse vs. Café)
