📖 1. The Core Text: San Lucas Clinic Expansion

Paragraph 2: HVAC Challenges
"However, we have faced some challenges recently regarding the HVAC system. This morning, I inspected the consultation rooms and found a problem with the air exchange rate. I have already spoken to the engineers, but they haven't fixed the issue yet."

Paragraph 3: The Crisis (Power Outage)
"In my career, I have seen many power outages, but I never saw anything like what happened yesterday. The backup generator failed during a medical imaging test. We **have already updated** the technical report, and the **stakeholders requested** a meeting for tomorrow."

📚 2. Vocabulary & Technical Terms

  • Regarding: Sobre / No que diz respeito a.
  • Faced: Encarar / Enfrentar.
  • Issue: Problema / Questão técnica.
  • Air exchange rate: Taxa de renovação do ar.
  • Power outage: Queda/Falta de energia.
  • Backup generator: Gerador de emergência.
  • Stakeholders: Partes interessadas / Sócios.
  • Medical imaging test: Exame de imagem.

🗣️ 3. Pronunciation & Grammar Notes

  • Career (/kəˈrɪər/): Ênfase no final (ca-REER).
  • Outages (/ˈaʊ.tɪ.dʒɪz/): Plural de queda de energia.
  • Find / Found / Found: Irregular (encontrar).
  • See / Saw / Seen: Experiência acumulada.
