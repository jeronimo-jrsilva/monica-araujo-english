Class XX: Pronunciation, Fluency & Technical Structure Drilling

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-05-01
  • Topic: Pronunciation, Fluency & Technical Structure Drilling

Mônica, pratique a leitura destas frases focando na fluidez e na conexão entre as palavras. O objetivo é ler cada frase sem pausas internas.

  1. I have worked on many healthcare projects since 2010.

  2. If the project followed the rules, it would be much safer.

  3. Have you already spoken to the subcontractors about the blueprints?

  4. Last year, my team designed the new wing of the San Lucas Clinic.

  5. The backup generator failed during a medical imaging test yesterday.

  6. I haven’t finished the technical report regarding the HVAC system yet.

  7. If I were the manager, I would change the inclusion policy immediately.

  8. In my career, I have seen many power outages in different hospitals.

  9. We finished the blueprints in June and the construction started right after.

  10. They have just found some errors in the air exchange rate calculations.

  11. If the room were sterile, the doctors would start the surgery now.

  12. Has she implemented the LEED standards in the maternity ward yet?

  13. I have already inspected the consultation rooms this morning.

  14. We have collaborated with this hospital for over five years.

  15. If the budget were higher, we would use sustainable materials.

  16. The stakeholders requested a meeting for tomorrow to discuss the issue.

  17. I have never dealt with such a tight deadline in my entire career.

  18. Yesterday, the engineers inspected the ventilation system in the ward.

  19. If you update the technical report, the board will be satisfied.

  20. I have already found a solution for the air exchange rate problem.

Dicas de Fluidez:

  • “Worked-on” -> /wɜːrkt-ɒn/

  • “Followed-the” -> /ˈfɒloʊd-ðə/

  • “Found-a” -> /faʊnd-ə/

  • “Spoken-to” -> /ˈspoʊkən-tuː/

  • “Tight-deadline” -> /taɪt-ˈdedlaɪn/
