Aula 25: Relative Clauses & Identifying with One/Ones

⚡ Warm-up: Passive Voice Review

Review from Class 24 (The “Report” Tone): - “We delivered the project yesterday.” ->The project was delivered yesterday.- “The technicians follow the safety standards.” ->Safety standards are followed by the technicians.- “Mônica chose the sustainable material.” ->The sustainable material was chosen by Mônica.### 🔍 Contrast: Safety vs. Security -Safety:Passive protection (safety standards, safety equipment). -Security:Active protection (guards, alarms, active systems).

🚀 Grammar 1: Relative Clauses (The Connectors)

Relative Clauses connect ideas and create fluid technical descriptions.

🔍 Key Pronouns:

-WHO (People):For professionals or team members. - Ex: “The specialistwhocalculates the load-bearing capacity…” -WHICH (Things):For objects, materials, laws or documents. - Ex: “The standardwhichregulates hospital lighting…” -THAT (People/Things):Versatile, used mainly in information essential for the sentence. - Ex: “The only materialthatmeets the budget is vinyl.” -WHERE (Places):For rooms, sectors or construction sites. - Ex: “The areawherethe vaccines are stored must have backup power.”

🚀 Grammar 2: The Power of ONE / ONES

Used to substitute a noun already mentioned, avoiding monotony.

🛠 Key Examples:

-Identifying:”Which layout do you prefer?” -> “I prefer theoneswith the open floor plan.” -Contrasting:”These fixtures are good, but theoneswe used in the previous project were better.” -The “Special” One:”Theonewho leads the BIM coordination is Mônica.”

🛠 Practical Drills (Classroom Results)

  1. “We have a contractor. He is responsible for the foundations.” ->The contractor WHO is responsible for the foundations.2. “I am looking for the blueprint. It shows the plumbing system.” ->I am looking for the blueprint WHICH shows the plumbing system.3. “This is the wing. We will install the new HVAC system there.” ->This is the wing WHERE we will install the new HVAC system.4. “There were many suppliers. Theonesthatoffered the best price were chosen.”

🗣 Professional Vocabulary & Phonetic Focus

-The day before yesterday:Anteontem. -Plumbing:Encanamento (Attention to the silent ‘b’). -Suppliers:Fornecedores. -Previous:Anterior. -Preview:Previsão. -Store / Storage:Armazenar / Armazenamento. -Which(/wɪtʃ/) vsWitch(/wɪtʃ/) - Homophones. -One(/wʌn/) vsWon(/wʌn/) - Homophones.
