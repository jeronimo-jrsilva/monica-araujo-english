🎯 Resumo da Realização

Nesta aula, avançamos na infraestrutura de saúde com foco em sistemas de controle ambiental (HVAC) e na introdução da Causative Form. Esta estrutura gramatical é essencial para o papel executivo da Mônica, permitindo expressar a delegação e contratação de serviços técnicos ("manda fazer").

⚙️ 2. Grammar: Causative Form (The Manager's Language)

Estrutura: Subject + Have/Get + Object + Past Participle

🛠️ Practice Drillings (Transformations):

  • Direct: "I will clean the filters." → Causative: "I will have the filters cleaned."
  • Direct: "The engineer checked the pressure." → Causative: "The engineer had the pressure checked."
  • Direct: "We must install the dampers." → Causative: "We must have the dampers installed."
  • Direct: "The client is testing the generator." → Causative: "The client is having the generator tested."
  • Direct: "I bought the HEPA filters." → Causative: "I had the HEPA filters bought/ordered."

📝 3. Mixed Structures & Logic

  • Executive Report: "I had the negative pressure rooms inspected because the nurse said there was a leak."
  • Existence (Review): There was / There were (Havia / Haviam).
  • Leak: Vazamento.
