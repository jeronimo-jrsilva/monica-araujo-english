Class 33: Sentence Linkers (Executive Depth)

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-05-30
  • Topic: Executive Reporting: Sentence Linkers (Addition, Contrast & Cause)

🎯 Executive Summary

Nesta aula, iniciamos a trilha de Sentence Linkers, focando em conectores de Adição, Contraste e Causa/Efeito. O objetivo foi elevar a sofisticação do discurso executivo, permitindo a conexão de ideias técnicas de forma fluida em reuniões e relatórios de conformidade.

🔗 1. Addition: Expanding Ideas

Usados para adicionar informações sem repetir "and".

Term (EN)Definition / Phonetic
In addition / AdditionallyAlém disso (Início de frase). / Ad-í-xonly
Furthermore / MoreoverAlém do mais / Ademais. / fãr-ther-moar / moar-ÔU-vrr
As well asAssim como / Bem como.
Not only... but also...Não apenas... mas também...

🗣️ Practical Drills:

  • "The project is on schedule. Furthermore, we stayed 5% under budget."
  • "The construction of the house is finished. Furthermore, we did some heavy cleaning."
  • "We installed the HEPA filters. Moreover, we checked the air exchange rate in the surgical wing."
  • "The new hospital has a maternity ward as well as an advanced pediatric ICU."
  • "We need not only the hard hats but also the high-visibility vests."

🔄 2. Contrast: Handling Contradictions

Usados para introduzir imprevistos, exceções ou oposições.

Term (EN)Definition / Context
HoweverNo entanto / Porém (O coringa corporativo).
Nonetheless / NeverthelessNo entanto / Mesmo assim (Ainda mais formal).
Although / Even thoughEmbora / Apesar de.
Despite / In spite ofApesar de (Seguido de substantivo ou ING).

🗣️ Practical Drills:

  • "The HVAC system is highly efficient. However, the installation cost is prohibitive."
  • "Although the scaffolding was inspected yesterday, we found a minor compliance issue today."
  • "Despite the delay in the ductwork delivery, the site remains operational."
  • "We have a shortage of PPE. Nevertheless, we must enforce the safety protocols."

📉 3. Cause & Effect: Explaining Decisions

Usados para justificar aprovações, atrasos ou mudanças de escopo.

  • "The foundation was compromised. Therefore, we had to halt the concrete pouring."
  • "Due to the heavy rain, the external painting was postponed."
  • "The supply chain broke down. As a result, we are sourcing materials locally."

🏗️ 4. Executive Transformation

🔴 Versão A (Basic): "We went to the site today and we checked the scaffolding. The scaffolding was okay but we saw a worker without a helmet. So we stopped the work because safety is important. And we bought new helmets."

🟢 Versão B (Executive Gold): "Initially, we conducted a site inspection as well as a scaffolding check. Although the structure was compliant, we identified a worker without the mandatory hard hat. Consequently, operations were temporarily halted due to our strict safety policy. Furthermore, we had new PPE distributed immediately."
