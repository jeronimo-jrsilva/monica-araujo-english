Class 15: Present Perfect & Project Status (Drilling Focus)

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-04-28
  • Topic: Practical Drills and Nuanced Status Checks (Already vs. Yet).

🎯 Resumo da Realização

Sessão intensiva de Drilling para consolidar o Present Perfect. Focamos na correção da homework anterior e na prática oral de transformação de autoridade. Exploramos as nuances entre Already (surpresa/rapidez) e Yet (expectativa/pendência) em cenários de obra e arquitetura.

🏗️ 1. Drilling Session: Present Perfect

🎯 Experience Transformation (Authority)

  • "I have finished the clinic layout."
  • "I have designed a hospital in Luanda."
  • "We have renovated the laboratory."

🎯 Duration (Since vs. For)

  • "I have been an architect since 2008."
  • "We have used this software for two weeks."

🏥 2. Scenario & Nuances: Already vs. Yet

Praticamos como responder ao cliente sobre o status do projeto:

  • "I have already finished the floor plan." (Pronto!)
  • "I haven't received the budget approval yet." (Ainda falta).

⚠️ Common Confusions

  • Already: Quando algo acontece antes do esperado. "The concrete is dry already!"
  • Yet: Expectativa não atendida. "The permits aren't ready yet."

🚀 3. Vocabulary Expansion

  • Troubleshoot: Identificar e resolver problemas.
  • Stakeholders: Partes interessadas no projeto.
  • Outage: Interrupção de serviço (energia/água).
  • Redundancy: Redundância (sistemas de backup).
