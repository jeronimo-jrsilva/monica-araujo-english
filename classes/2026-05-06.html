Lesson 20: Spatial Drills & Hospital Flows

Wednesday, May 6, 2026

Circulation & Direction

Prepositions of Movement

  • TO / FROM: Destination & Origin (From the lab to the office).
  • THROUGH: Crossing a 3D space (Through the automatic doors).
  • ALONG: Following a line/path (Along the main corridor).
  • ACROSS: Crossing a 2D surface (Across the lobby).
  • INTO / OUT OF: Entering or leaving (Into the sterile zone / Out of the room).
  • UP / DOWN: Different levels (Up to the rooftop / Down the ramp).

Technical Vocabulary

Stretcher: Maca
Sample: Amostra
Waste: Lixo / Resíduos
Signage: Sinalização
Reach: Alcançar / Chegar a
Strip: Faixa (no chão/corredor)

Precision Drills

Emergency Protocol: "The team must take the critically ill patients to the emergency elevator and out of the building."

Logistics: "The waste goes out of the room to the disposal area."
