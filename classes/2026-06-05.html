Topic: Executive Planning & Contrast Fundamentals

Consolidating connectors of addition/contrast and executive planning vocabulary.

1. Sound Lab & Vocabulary

  • Schedule (/ˈskedʒuːl/): Agenda / Agendar.
  • Surgery (/ˈsɜːrdʒəri/): Cirurgia.
  • Surgical: Cirúrgico.
  • Challenge (/ˈtʃælɪndʒ/): Desafio.
  • Furthermore: Além disso...
  • However / Although: Contudo, no entanto.

2. Contrast Fundamentals

Rule: Although + Clause | Despite + Noun/-ing

  • "Although it was raining..." → "Despite the rain..."
  • "Although the budget is tight..." → "Despite the tight budget..."

3. Mind & Thoughts

  • To think / thought: Pensar / Pensou.
  • Read my mind: Ler minha mente.
