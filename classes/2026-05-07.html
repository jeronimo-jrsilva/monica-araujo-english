📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-05-07
  • Topic: Advanced Prepositions & Technical Specifications (Materials & Finishes)

Objetivos da Aula

  1. Advanced Prepositions: Introduzir preposições complexas de limite e direção (Beyond, Toward, Past, Within).

  2. Technical Specs: Vocabulário de acabamentos e materiais hospitalares (Coating, Anti-microbial, Seamless, Flooring).

  3. Descriptive Precision: Descrever a composição de um ambiente técnico com precisão.

Technical Vocabulary: Materials & Finishes

Como especificar materiais em inglês técnico:

  • Coating: Revestimento / Pintura técnica.

    • “Coating is necessary to protect this wall.”

  • Anti-microbial: Antimicrobiano.

  • Seamless: Sem emendas / Monolítico (ex: Seamless flooring).

    • “The surgical rooms must have seamless floor.”

  • Non-slip / Slip-resistant: Antiderrapante.

  • Cladding: Revestimento externo / Fachada.

  • Joint: Junta / Emenda.

New Content: Advanced Prepositions (Complex Relationships)

Além de direção e lugar, precisamos de preposições que descrevam relações e processos:

  1. BEYOND / WITHIN (Limite): Além de / Dentro do limite.
    • “The sterile storage is located beyond the second airlock.”
    • “The pressure must remain within the safety range of 2.0 bar.”
    • “Is the expansion area beyond the parking lot or within the main site?”
  2. AGAINST (Encostado em / Contra): Contato físico com superfície vertical ou resistência.
    • “Place the stainless steel cabinets against the back wall.”
    • “The door must be fire-rated and resistant against high temperatures.”
    • “The architectural team leaned the blueprints against the whiteboard.”
  3. THROUGHOUT (Por todo / Em toda a extensão): Espalhamento total.
    • “The anti-microbial coating is applied throughout the surgical wing.”
    • “We need to install seamless flooring throughout the entire ICU.”
    • “The ventilation system ensures fresh air flow throughout the building.”
  4. VIA / BY WAY OF (Via / Por meio de): Rota ou método.
    • “The medical oxygen is supplied via the copper pipes in the wall.”
    • “Staff can access the cafeteria by way of the technical corridor.”
    • “The data signal is transmitted via fiber optic cables throughout the hospital.”
  5. PAST / TOWARD (Referencial): Passando por / Em direção a.
    • “Go past the nurse station and walk toward the radiology department.”
    • “The stretcher was moved past the lobby and toward the emergency exit.”
    • “Look past the main entrance; you will see the sign toward the parking garage.”

Intensive Drills: Technical Scenarios

Mônica, responda com precisão usando as novas preposições e vocabulário:

Scenario A: Material Specification

  1. Where do we apply the anti-microbial paint? (It is applied throughout the operating rooms).

  2. Where should the furniture be placed? (Place it against the walls to clear the corridor).

  3. What is the limit for the humidity? (It must stay within 40% and 60%).

Scenario B: Layout & Navigation

  1. How do I reach the pharmacy from here? (Go past the lobby and walk toward the cafeteria).

  2. Is the technical room before the lab? (No, it is located beyond the lab area).

  3. How does the water reach the sinks? (It comes via the PEX pipes).

Roleplay: The Project Review

Simulação de revisão de projeto com o Diretor Técnico:

  • Director: “Where is the oxygen tank located?”

  • Mônica: “It is within the technical shed, beyond the parking lot.”

  • Director: “And the flooring in the sterile zone?”

  • Mônica: “We specified a seamless coating throughout the area for hygiene.”

  • Director: “How do surgeons access the OR?”

  • Mônica: “They go past the locker rooms and toward the scrub sinks.”

Vocabulary & Teacher’s Feedback

  • Ensures: Garante / Certifica.

  • Flow vs. Flaw: Fluxo (/floʊ/) vs. Falha (/flɔː/).

  • Phrase vs. Sentence: Frase (curta/idiomática) vs. Sentença (completa/gramatical).

  • Entire: Inteiro / Todo (Ex: “Throughout the entire ICU”).

  • Stretcher: Maca.

  • Cleaning vs. Construction:

    • “During construction, anti-microbial materials are used in floors, ceilings and coatings. Anti-microbial products are also used to clean stuff.”
