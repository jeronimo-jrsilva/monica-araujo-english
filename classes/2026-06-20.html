New Key Words & Visuals

Lead-lining & Shielding

  • Lead-lining: A protective layer of lead installed in walls to prevent radiation transmission.

  • Shielding: Materials or structures used to block, absorb, or redirect radiation or physical impact.

  • Example: You must install lead-lining in the walls of the oncology wing.

  • Example: The inspector verified the radiation shielding in the X-Ray room.

Snags & Punch list

  • Snags: Minor defects, scratch marks, or unfinished details at the end of a construction project.

  • Scratch - arranhar

  • Stretch - esticar / alongar

  • Punch list: A document listing key tasks, small repairs, and touch-ups required before final project delivery.

  • Example: We need to iron out the snags before the final audit.

  • Example: The contractor is working on the final punch list this week.

Outlet

  • Outlet: A wall socket where electrical devices are plugged in to receive power.
  • Example: The project requires extra electrical outlets in the ICU rooms.
