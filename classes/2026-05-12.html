🎯 Resumo da Realização

Introdução à Passive Voice (Voz Passiva) em contextos técnicos de arquitetura e relatórios hospitalares. Foco na objetividade ("The tiles were installed") e revisão de intensificadores. Prática de particípios passados irregulares e contrastes de pronúncia essenciais.

🚀 Technical Grammar: The Passive Voice

Usada para soar mais formal e focado no processo do que no agente:

  • Present Passive: "NBR standards are followed."
  • Past Passive: "The tiles were installed yesterday."
  • Structure: Object + BE (am/is/are/was/were) + Past Participle (3rd column).

🏗️ Material Description & Technical Verbs

Verb (Past Participle) Context / Sentence
Built (Build)"The surgery room was built in February."
Bought (Buy)"The hospital was bought by the D'Or Group."
Chosen (Choose)"Our team was chosen for the project among 10 options."
Worn (Wear)"PPE must be worn by all workers."

🧠 Teacher's Feedback & Phonetics

  • Among: Entre (vários / muitos).
  • Confusing Words (Sound Contrasts):
    • Worn (/wɔːrn/) vs. Warm (/wɔːrm/)
    • Warn (/wɔːrn/) vs. Worm (/wɜːrm/)
    • Arm (/ɑːrm/) vs. Worm (/wɜːrm/)
