🏗️ 2. Prática de Aula (Grammar & Drills)

🎯 Present Perfect & Nuances

  • "They have just postponed the meeting about the new imaging room."
  • "Have you finished the workshop proposal for the board yet?"
  • "We have collaborated with this hospital for over five years."

🎯 Conditionals (Review)

  • "If the room were sterile, the doctors would start the surgery now."
  • "If I were the manager, I would change the inclusion policy."

🗣️ Pronunciation & Vocabulary Notes

  • Proposal: /prəˈpəʊzl/ (pro-PÔU-zal).
  • Issue: /ˈɪʃuː/ (Í-chuu).
  • Policy (Política) vs. Police (Polícia).
  • Postponed: Adiado.
  • Sleep: Slept (Past).
