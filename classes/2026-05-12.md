## ⚡ Warm-up: Too vs. Enough (Review)
*   "The budget is **too expensive** for this project."
*   "We don't have **enough luminaires** for the reception."
*   "The lux level isn't **bright enough** for the surgery room."

## 🚀 Grammar: The Passive Voice (Deep Dive)
*Em contextos técnicos e corporativos, a Voz Passiva é usada para soar mais **objetivo**, **formal** e focado no **resultado**.*

**Estrutura:** Substantivo + Verbo BE (conjugado) + Particípio Passado (3ª coluna).

### 1. Present Passive (General Facts / Standard Procedures)
*   **Active:** "Our team follows the NBR standards."
*   **Passive:** "NBR standards **are followed** (by our team)."
*   **Active:** "The system controls the temperature."
*   **Passive:** "The temperature **is controlled** by the system."

### 2. Past Passive (Completed Tasks / Reports)
*   **Active:** "The contractor installed the tiles yesterday."
*   **Passive:** "The tiles **were installed** yesterday."
*   **Active:** "The client approved the lighting layout."
*   **Passive:** "The layout **was approved** by the client."

### 3. Practice Drills
*   "The technicians tested the emergency lights." -> **The emergency lights were tested by the technicians.**
*   "Mônica designs the custom furniture." -> **The custom furniture is designed by Mônica.**

## 🏗️ Technical Specs: Material Description & Verbs
*Descrevendo propriedades e processos:*

*   **Build (Built):** "The emergency room **was built** in February."
*   **Buy (Bought):** "The hospital **was bought** by the D'Or Group."
*   **Choose (Chosen):** "Our team **was chosen** for the project by the directors in February **among** 10 other options."
*   **Wear (Worn):** "PPE must **be worn** by all workers."

## 👄 Pronunciation & Vocabulary
*   **Technicians:** /tekˈnɪʃənz/ (Atenção à tônica no NI).
*   **Custom:** Personalizado.
*   **Among:** Entre (vários/muitos).
*   **I believe you:** Eu acredito em você.

**Confusing Words (Sound Contrasts):**
*   **Worn** (/wɔːrn/) - Gasto / Usado (Particípio de Wear).
*   **Warn** (/wɔːrn/) - Aviso / Advertência.
*   **Worm** (/wɜːrm/) - Verme / Minhoca.
*   **Warm** (/wɔːrm/) - Quente / Morno.
*   **Arm** (/ɑːrm/) - Braço.
