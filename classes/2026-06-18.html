1. The Site Log: What Happened? (Past Simple Drill)

Foco: Fluidez no relato de eventos passados e correção fonética.

  1. The contractor brought the wrong insulation last Friday.
    • (sent / broken tiles / Wednesday)
    • (found / gas leak / morning)
    • Vocab: Bought (comprou) vs Brought (trouxe). Send/Sent (enviar).
  2. The board signed the permit for the oncology wing.
    • (reviewed / budget / ICU renovation)
    • (rejected / blueprints / maternity ward)
    • Vocab: Review (revisar), Reject (recusar).
  3. I met the subcontractor at the main entrance.
    • Vocab: Meet / Met (encontrar/conhecer).
  4. The team ran out of fire-rated glass yesterday.
    • Vocab: Run / Ran (correr/esgotar). Fire-rated glass (vidro corta-fogo). Copper pipes (canos/dutos de cobre).
  5. We found a major plumbing issue in the basement.
    • Vocab: Issue (problema, questão, contratempo). Basement (porão). Penthouse (cobertura).
    • Phonetic Drill:
      • /ˈfeɪljər/ - Failure (falha)
      • /feɪl/ - Fail (falhar)
      • /faɪl/ - File (arquivo)
  6. The client spoke to the lead architect about the costs.
    • Vocab: Lead architect (arquiteto líder). Complain to (reclamar para).
  7. They built the temporary wall in three hours.
    • Vocab: Build / Built (construir). Elevator door (porta do elevador).
  8. The inspector chose a different sample of marble.
    • Vocab: Choose / Chose (escolher). Sample (amostra). Marble (mármore). Take / Took (pegar/levar).
    • Phonetic Drill: Chose (/tʃoʊz/) vs Shoes (/ʃuːz/).
  9. I thought the project was ahead of schedule.
    • Vocab: Think / Thought (pensar/achar). Ahead (à frente). Feel / Felt (sentir).
  10. The storm broke the scaffolding on the north side.
    • Vocab: Storm (tempestade). Scaffolding (andaimes). Side (lado/direção). Break / Broke (quebrar). Strong (forte). Damage (danificar). Solar panels (painéis solares). Roof (telhado).
