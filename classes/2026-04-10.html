Class 03: 3rd Person Singular & Company Policy

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-04-10
  • Topic: Present Simple (3rd Person Singular)

🏗️ Affirmative Form (3rd Person Singular)

Rule: Subject (He/She/It) + Verb + -s / -es / -ies

  • "She goes to the beach every morning."
  • "It is named Monica Araujo Architecture Studio."
  • "It is located in Brasilia's downtown."
  • "It is 15 years old (company age)."

🚫 Negative Form (Doesn't)

Rule: Subject + doesn't + Verb (Base form)

  • "It doesn't take big constructions."
  • "It doesn't take calls from clients on weekends."
  • "It doesn't take bribes."
  • "It doesn't offer training or courses."

❓ Interrogative Form (Does)

Rule: Does + Subject + Verb (Base form)?

  • "Does she live in Brasilia?"
  • "Does she like ice cream?"
  • "Where does she work?"

📚 Vocabulary & Expressions

  • Downtown: Centro da cidade (área comercial).
  • Named: Nomeado / Chamado.
  • Located: Localizado.
  • Bribes: Subornos / Propinas.
  • Big constructions: Grandes obras / construções.
  • Go ahead: Vá em frente.
  • Trivia: Curiosidades / Fatos.

📝 Observations

  • Age & Location: Remember to use "It is" instead of "It has" for age and location (e.g., *It's 15 years old*).
