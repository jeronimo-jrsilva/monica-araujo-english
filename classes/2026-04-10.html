Class 03: Present Simple (3rd Person Singular - Aff/Neg/Int)

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-04-10
  • Topic: Present Simple (3rd Person Singular - Aff/Neg/Int)

Objetivo

Dominar a flexão de verbos na 3ª pessoa do singular (He/She/It) em contextos de especificações técnicas e comportamento de materiais.

1. Affirmative Form (3rd Person Singular)

Rule: Subject (He/She/It) + Verb + -s / -es / -ies

Concept Example
Habit She goes to the beach every morning.
Identity It is named Monica Araujo Architecture Studio.
Location It is located in Brasilia’s downtown.
Experience It is 15 years old (company age).

2. Negative Form (Doesn’t)

Rule: Subject + doesn’t + Verb (Base form)

Context (Company Policy) Phrase
Scale It doesn’t take big constructions.
Boundaries It doesn’t take calls from clients on weekends.
Ethics It doesn’t take bribes.
Services It doesn’t offer training or courses.

3. Interrogative Form (Does)

Rule: Does + Subject + Verb (Base form)?

  • Does she live in Brasilia? (Yes, she lives / No, she doesn’t live).

  • Does she like ice cream?

  • Where does she work?

Glossário & Expressões

  • Downtown: Centro da cidade (área comercial).

  • Named: Nomeado/Chamado.

  • Located: Localizado.

  • Bribes: Subornos/Propinas.

  • Big constructions: Grandes obras/construções.

  • Go ahead: Vá em frente.

  • Trivia: Curiosidades/Fatos inúteis.

Feedback e Observações

  • Pontos Fortes: Boa compreensão da diferença entre 1ª e 3ª pessoa.

  • Dificuldades: Uso do “have” vs “is” para idade e localização (corrigido: It’s 15 years old).

  • Ações para Aula 04: Praticar perguntas complexas com Wh- Questions (Where, What, Who, When, Why, How).
