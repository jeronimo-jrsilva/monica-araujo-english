1. THE CAUSATIVE MACHINE (Infrastructure Failures)
Delegate the solution:

- "The security cameras are blurry."
- "The blueprints are outdated."
- "The fire alarm is beeping."
- "The paint is peeling in the lobby."

---
2. THE TIME MACHINE (Past vs. Present Perfect)
Reflex response (Check for time markers):

- "You (finish) the report yesterday?"
- "You (visit) the site recently?"
- "We (fix) the leak two hours ago."
- "I (never / see) this type of HVAC."
- "The board (approve) the budget last week."

---
3. THE COMPLIANCE MACHINE (Modals: MUST / SHOULD / CAN'T)
React to the violation:

- "A worker is without a hard hat."
- "The emergency exit is blocked."
- "A visitor wants to enter the ICU."
- "The lead time is too long."

---
4. THE LOGICAL MACHINE (Conditionals)
Instant "What-if":

- "If the audit starts now..."
- "If we had more budget..."
- "If the elevator fails tomorrow..."
- "If I were the CEO..."

---
5. FINAL CHALLENGE: THE MULTI-TASK CEO
Manage 3 simultaneous phone calls:
1. Problem (Causative)
2. Update (Past Simple)
3. Warning (Conditional)
