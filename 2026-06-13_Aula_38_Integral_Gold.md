# 🎓 Aula 38: Monica Araujo (13/06)

> [!abstract] Transbordo da Aula 37
> Consolidação de Relative Clauses e aplicação prática de Phrasal Verbs e Causative Form em um cenário de Auditoria Governamental.

## 🏗️ 1. Precision Review: Relative Clauses (Identity & Function)
*Objective: Defining technical roles and documents with precision.*

**Precision Drill (WHO / WHICH / THAT):**

1. **A Subcontractor**: "A person **who** repairs the HVAC in the ICU."
2. **A Blueprint**: "A document **which** shows the structural design."
3. **A Bottleneck**: "A situation **that** delays the project progress."
4. **The Board**: "The group **who** approves the budget."
5. **A Punch List**: "A list **which** contains the remaining small tasks."
6. **A Stakeholder**: "Anyone **who** is affected by the project."

---

## ⚡ 2. Review Drill: The Causative Matrix
*Quick-fire delegation exercise (HAVE + SOMETHING + DONE).*

| Scenario | Action (Delegated) | Key Terms |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hospital Elevator** | "I'll **have** it **fixed**." | `Stuck` (preso/travado) |
| **Floor Tiles** | "I'll **have** them **replaced**." | `Cracked` (rachado) |
| **Rejected Budget** | "I'll **have** it **revised**." | `Budget` (orçamento) |
| **X-Ray Shielding** | "I'll **have** it **checked**." | `Shielding` (blindagem) |
| **Biohazard Signs** | "I'll **have** them **reprinted**." | `Biohazard` (risco biológico) |

---

## 🏆 3. The Final Challenge: The Pre-Audit Briefing
*Goal: Brief your team for the "Government Audit".*

**Context:** The government audit starts in 2 hours. Issues remain with X-Ray shielding and biohazard signage.

**Key Phrases for Briefing:**
- "Despite the delay, we will **carry out** the final inspection."
- "I have already **had** the shielding **checked**."
- "We need to **iron out** these details before they arrive."
- "Although the time is short, I'll **have** the signs **reprinted**."

---

## 📚 4. Vocabulary & Management Terms
- **To Brief:** Dar instruções/resumo (to provide essential information).
- **To Oversee:** Supervisionar (to supervise/manage).
- **Lead Time:** Prazo de entrega / Tempo de espera (time between start and finish).
- **Punch List:** Lista de pendências finais (remaining minor tasks).
- **Audit:** Auditoria.
