💡 Pronunciation & Usage Tips

  • Read vs. Read: No presente: "Ríid". No passado: "Red" (como a cor vermelha).
  • Said: Pronuncia-se "Sed" no passado, nunca "Sêid".
  • Was vs. Were: "I was tired" (Eu estava cansada) / "They were late" (Eles estavam atrasados).
