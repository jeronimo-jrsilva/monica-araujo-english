🏗️ Part 1: Professional Authority (10 Sentences)

Rewrite these sentences using the Present Perfect to sound more like an expert describing their background.

  1. I designed a clinic in 2018. → I have designed several clinics.
  2. I studied the RDC 50 regulations last year. → I have studied ...
  3. I used AutoCAD during college. → I have used ...
  4. I coordinated a construction site in 2022. → I have coordinated ...
  5. I implemented a medical gas system in that project. → I have implemented ...
  6. I lived in another city for 2 years. → I have lived ...
  7. I met with international contractors in March. → I have met ...
  8. I finished the pediatric wing floor plan. → I have finished ...
  9. I oversaw the installation of the backup generator. → I have overseen ...
  10. I worked as a specialized architect since I graduated. → I have worked ...

⚡ Part 2: Project Status (Just, Already, Yet)

Complete the dialogue between an Architect and a Client.

Client: Have you finished the electrical layout (1) __________?
Architect: No, I haven’t finished it (2) __________, but I have (3) __________ completed the plumbing draft.
Client: Great. Has the contractor called you?
Architect: Yes, he has (4) __________ called me. We spoke 2 minutes ago!
Client: And what about the backup generator?
Architect: The team has (5) __________ installed it. It’s ready for testing.
