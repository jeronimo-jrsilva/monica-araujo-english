Pronunciation and Usage Tips

  • Read vs. Read: No presente você diz “Ríid”. No passado você diz “Red” (como a cor vermelha).
  • Said: Não diga “Sêid”. A pronúncia correta no passado é “Sed”.
  • Went / Did: “I went to the office” / “I did my work”.
  • Was / Were: “I was tired” / “They were late”.
