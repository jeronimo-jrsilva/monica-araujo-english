Class 02: Present Simple (Negative & Interrogative)

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-04-09
  • Topic: Present Simple (Negative & Interrogative)

Objetivo

Dominar a negação e interrogação no Present Simple para descrever processos que não ocorrem ou questionar conformidades técnicas.

1. Negative Form (Don’t / Doesn’t)

Regra: Subject + do/does + NOT + Verb (Base form)

Contexto Profissional Frase Exemplo
Material This coating doesn’t meet the antimicrobial standard.
Workflow We don’t start the HVAC installation before the duct sealing.
Circulation The emergency exit doesn’t open to the main lobby.

2. Interrogative Form (Do / Does)

Regra: Do/Does + Subject + Verb (Base form)?

  • Does this recovery room have natural lighting?

  • Do the engineers follow the NBR-1890 protocol?

  • Why does the laboratory require a double-door airlock?

3. Quick Drill (Arquitetura)

  1. Afirmativa: The corridor connects to the ICU.

  2. Negativa: The corridor doesn’t connect to the ICU.

  3. Pergunta: Does the corridor connect to the ICU?

Glossário de Hoje

  • HVAC: Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (Climatização).

  • Coating: Revestimento.

  • Duct: Duto.

  • Airlock: Câmara de eclusa (proteção de pressão).
