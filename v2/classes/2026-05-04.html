Class 19: Prepositions of Place (Spatial Design)

📅 Lesson Info

  • Date: 2026-05-04
  • Topic: Prepositions of Place: In, On, At & Architectural Layouts.

🧠 1. Technical Grammar: The "Preposition Pyramid"

AT (Specific Points)

  • At the entrance (Na entrada).
  • At the construction site (No canteiro de obras).
  • At the corner of the room (No canto da sala).

ON (Surfaces & Floors)

  • On the floor / On the roof (No chão / No telhado).
  • On the second floor (No segundo andar).
  • On the wall (Na parede).
  • On the ceiling (No teto).

IN (Enclosed Spaces & Areas)

  • In the operating room (Na sala de cirurgia).
  • In the storage area (Na área de estoque).
  • In the blueprint (No desenho técnico).

🏗️ 2. Describe the Layout (Proximity)

  • Beside / Next to: Lado a lado. (Ex: The cabinet is next to the exam table).
  • Between: Entre dois pontos. (Ex: The hallway is between the triage and the office).
  • Opposite / In front of: De frente para. (Ex: The pharmacy is opposite the waiting area).
  • Under / Below: Embaixo / Abaixo. (Ex: Pipes run under the floor).

🗣️ 3. Vocabulary & Pronunciation

  • Blueprint: Planta baixa / Projeto técnico.
  • Fixture: Item fixo (pia, luminária).
  • Layout: Arranjo físico.
  • Site: Local (canteiro/clínica).
