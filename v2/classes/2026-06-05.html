1. Warm-up: Next Week’s Vision

Prompt: “Tell me about a project challenge for next week.” Target: Use “Additionally” or “Furthermore”.

Practice:

  • “I’ll schedule…”

  • “I will finish…”

  • “I will work in…” (until the weekend)

2. Sound Lab & Vocabulary nuances

  • Schedule /ˈskedʒuːl/: Agenda / Agendar.

  • Surgery /ˈsɜːrdʒəri/: Cirurgia.

  • Surgical / Cirurgic: Cirúrgico.

  • Craft: Fazer / “Fabricar” (com habilidade).

  • Challenge /ˈtʃælɪndʒ/: Desafio.

  • Hygienic /haɪˈdʒiːnɪk/: Higiênico.

  • Measure /ˈmeʒər/: Medida.

  • Furthermore: Além disso…

  • However / Although: Contudo, todavia, entretanto.

  • High / Low: Alto / Baixo.

    • Idiom: “Highs and lows” (Altos e baixos).

Mind & Thoughts:

  • To think / thought / thought: Pensar / Pensou.

  • I’m thinking…: “Tô pensando!”

  • Read my thoughts / Read my mind: Ler meus pensamentos / Ler minha mente.

  • “You are always on my mind”: Você está sempre em meus pensamentos.

3. Core Drill: Contrast Fundamentals

Rule: Although + [Clause] | Despite + [Noun/-ing]

Mechanical Drills:

  1. “Although it was raining…” -> “Despite the rain…”

  2. “Although the budget is tight…” -> “Despite the tight budget…”

    • Tight: Apertado.
    • Need / Needed: Precisar / Precisou.
    • Bought: Comprou.
    • Print / Printer: Imprimir / Impressora.
